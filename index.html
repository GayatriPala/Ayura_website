Our Story

AYURA began with a simple but powerful intention — to make premium aesthetic care accessible, comfortable, and deeply personalised. Instead of asking clients to visit a clinic, we wanted to bring luxury wellness directly to them.

What started as a small vision has grown into a complete home-aesthetic service experience. From skin and hair treatments to IV drips, every service at AYURA is designed to blend clinical expertise with spa-like comfort — right at your doorstep.

Our Vision • Approach • Philosophy

At AYURA, our vision is simple — to make advanced aesthetic care effortlessly accessible. We believe that premium skin, hair, and wellness treatments should reach you wherever you are, without compromising on expertise, luxury, or safety.

Our approach is built around personalised home care. From the moment you book a service, our experts create a customised treatment plan, arrive with hospital-grade hygiene standards, and deliver a seamless, comfortable experience right at your doorstep.

We believe true beauty is authentic. Our philosophy focuses on enhancing your natural features through safe, evidence-based treatments while respecting your privacy, time, and comfort — all within the warmth of your own home.
